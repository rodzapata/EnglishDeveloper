In English, contractions are commonly used to make speech sound more natural and flut.
you're is a contraction of you are.

This contractions is a combination of the pronoun you and the verb are. which is part of the
verb to be. Are is used here in the present affirmative to make a statement or ask a question.
this is a typical way to confirm someone's role or identity in English.
